Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How fast is your delivery service? +
Our standard delivery takes 30–45 minutes. Pro plan members enjoy express delivery under 30 minutes. We use real-time routing to get your food to you as quickly as possible.
Can I customize my order? +
Absolutely! You can adjust ingredients, remove allergens, change portion sizes, and add special instructions on every item. We accommodate most dietary requirements including vegan, gluten-free, and more.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash on delivery. All online payments are encrypted and secure.
How do subscription boxes work? +
Choose weekly or monthly delivery. Each box is curated with seasonal ingredients, chef-crafted recipes, and exclusive snacks. You can skip, pause, or cancel anytime with no fees.
Do you offer catering for large events? +
Yes! We cater events of any size — from 10 to 1,000+ guests. Our team handles everything from menu planning to on-site setup. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance to get a quote.
